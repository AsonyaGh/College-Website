About Us

On November 6, 2006 the Health Assistant (Clinical) Programme started in Wa in the Upper West Region. The establishment of the programme by the Ministry of Health (MOH) was borne out of genuine need initiated by Regional Health Directorate with Dr. E. A. Agongo as the Regional Director of Health Services (RDHS) and Regional Coordination Council (RCC) under the tutelage of the Regional Minister, Honourable Ambrose Dery.

Courses We Offer

For now, we offer two courses, which are:
1. Registered General Nursing, whish is a diploma course of Three (3) years duration and
2. Nurse Assistant Clinicals which is a certificate course of two (2) years duration
