RGN (Diploma) Program

The entry requirements comprise
I. Senior Secondary School Certificate of Examination (SSSCE) or West African Senior Secondary Certificate of Examination (WASSCE).
II. Credits in 6 subjects (English, Mathematics, Integrated Science and 3 electives which must be Arts, Science or Home Economics categories).
III. A combination of Senior Secondary Certificate of Education (SSCE) and West African Senior Secondary Certificate of Education (WASSCE) is not ACCEPTABLE except in the case of health service personnel.
IV. Besides the aforementioned the applicant must be 16 years and not more than 35 years with no criminal record and should be in good health

NAC (Certificate) Program

The entry requirements comprise
I. Senior Secondary School Certificate of Examination (SSSCE) or West African Senior Secondary Certificate of Examination (WASSCE).
II. Passes in 6 subjects (English, Mathematics, Integrated Science and then any 3 electives). III. A combination of Senior Secondary Certificate of Education (SSCE) and West African Senior Secondary Certificate of Education (WASSCE) is not ACCEPTABLE except in the case of health service personnel.
IV. Besides the aforementioned, the applicant must be 16 years and not more than 35 years with no criminal record and should be in good health
